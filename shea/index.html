About me

I am an aspiring software developer currently based in Bristol. After graduating with a first in Biological Sciences from the University of the West of England, I have spent the past two years living and working in Shanghai, China. Within a three-year period, I have worked in radiation laboratories, gained intermediate level qualifications in Mandarin and begun to learn the skills required to have a successful entry into the technology industry. Having already applied training in both HTML and CSS I am beginning to learn the utilisation of JavaScript, Node.JS and Angular. Outside of work my interests range from reading and playing guitar to attending football matches and travelling.
